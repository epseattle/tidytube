Watch Later — Workaround

YouTube's API blocks all access to Watch Later playlists. This is a Google-imposed restriction that no third-party app can work around directly.

Here's how you can still manage your Watch Later videos with TidyTube:

1

Export your Watch Later list

Use Google Takeout to download your YouTube data, which includes your Watch Later playlist as a CSV/JSON file.

Open Google Takeout →

Select only YouTube and YouTube Music → choose your format → export.

2

Create a "My Watch Later" playlist

TidyTube can create a regular playlist that works just like Watch Later — but fully manageable.

3

Use TidyTube going forward

Save new videos to your "My Watch Later" playlist instead of YouTube's built-in one. You'll have full batch control — select, delete, move, and copy freely.
